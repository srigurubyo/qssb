About Quantum Springs

Building Growth Through Innovation

Quantum Springs is a 25‑year‑old vertically integrated group headquartered in Teluk Intan, Perak. From our 30‑acre rubber nursery and heavy‑machinery services to turnkey construction and custom‑built software, we deliver seamless, end‑to‑end solutions that empower businesses to scale efficiently.

CORE VALUES

  • Innovation — Continuous R&D and technology integration keep us ahead of industry trends.
